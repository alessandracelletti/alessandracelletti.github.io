My research activity focusses on Dynamical Systems and Celestial Mechanics.

The main topics are the following:

  • Kolmogorov-Arnold-Moser (KAM) theory in conservative and conformally symplectic systems
  • Development of optimal estimates in the framework of the Kolmogorov-Arnold-Moser (KAM) theory and Nekhoroshev theorem
  • Dynamics of nearly-integrable systems with dissipation
  • Stability results for the spin-orbit interaction, the Lagrangian equilibrium points, the J2 problem and the three-body problem in Celestial Mechanics
  • Investigation of the complex singularities of KAM surfaces
  • Capture into resonance and construction of librational invariant surfaces
  • Development of numerical methods for the computation of the break-down threshold of invariant (rotational and librational) surfaces
  • Orbital determination and space debris dynamics
  • Applications of dynamical systems to neurobiology
  • Space Manifold Dynamics (SMD)
  • Construction of periodic orbits and investigation of the behaviour of resonances in Celestial Mechanics
